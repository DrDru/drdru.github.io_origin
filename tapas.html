寒さむくて死しにそう
Play audio

寒さむい cold
寒さむくて is the て-form of 寒さむい. て-form has several uses. In this case it allows to link 寒さむい to what's next.
死しぬ to die
死しに Dying
そう to have the appearance
死しにそう about to die

This expression often is often used in conversation and could be translated as :

> "I'm so cold I could die" or "I'm freezing to death".

撃うつのはやめて
Play audio

撃うつ to shoot
止やめる to stop
Appending のは transforms the verb 撃うつ into a noun so it becomes 'shooting' or 'the act of shooting'.
やめて is the て-form of 止やめる which in this case is used to express a command. It's rather soft compared with やめろ which would be direct / rude.

"Stop the act of shooting"

> Stop shooting

武器ぶきを使つかうのは良よくない
Play audio

武器ぶき weapon
使つかう to use
良よく good
ない is the negation so 良よくない is 'not good'

武器ぶきを使つかう to use a weapon
Here again のは transforms the proposition into a noun.
"The act of using this weapon is not good."

> Using this weapon would be bad
