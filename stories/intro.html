Welcome to DrDru's lab

This site has two goals :
  • prove that you can learn japanese without using your mother tongue
  • make it a fun experience

Before starting make sure you're comfortable with hiragana and katakana.
Not everything will be clear the first time you see it and it's OK. Keep scrolling. It will become clear eventually.
Despite being in the form of stories the Japanese used is beginner Japanese and may not reflect the way native speakers would express themselves.
As the vocabulary and grammar expands it becomes closer to native speech.
